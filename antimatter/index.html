Antimatter

What is Antimatter?

Antimatter is a mirror reflection of subatomic regular matter. Each particle shares an identical mass with its everyday counterpart, but carries an exactly inverted electrical charge. When the two collide, they instantly annihilate transforming matter back into pure energetic photons.
